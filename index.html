The Club

Every discipline.
One community.

Most clubs make you pick a lane. Lifters lift, runners run, cyclists ride — and the people training across all of it end up training alone. Hybrid Club exists to change that.

We're a Sydney-based community for athletes who train across weightlifting, running, cycling, swimming and triathlon. Whether you're chasing a first parkrun, a bigger squat, or an Ironman finish line, you train alongside people who get it. Every session has options for every level — the standard is showing up, not keeping up.

01

Lift

Structured strength sessions built around the compound lifts. Muscle that performs, not just poses.

02

Run

Track intervals, tempo work and long runs. Every pace group, nobody dropped.

03

Ride

Weekend bunch rides with regroups. Coffee mandatory, watts optional.

04

Swim & Tri

Squad swims, open water and triathlon prep — from first splash to race day.

I'm passionate about getting better in every discipline — swimming, cycling and running.
