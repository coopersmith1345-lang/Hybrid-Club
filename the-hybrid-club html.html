About

One club.
Both disciplines.

Most clubs make you choose. Runners run, lifters lift, and the gap between them is where hybrid athletes get left training alone. The Hybrid Club exists to close that gap.

We're a Sydney-based community for people who refuse to pick a lane — built by a competitive triathlete and a registered nurse who believe strength and endurance belong in the same program. Every week we train together across the gym, the track, the road and the water, with sessions programmed so each side makes the other better.
